Hi! Welcome to Streak Coding


Round 1: Trace for Pass

There are 3 rounds, in the first round you will be given a C++ or Java program (your choice)
You'll have to find the output of the program manually using pen and paper.
The faster you find the correct answer, the better head start you gain to the next round
Added to that faster teams would get more points than the relatively slower teams and wrong submissions result in a reduction in score
All of this score would be maintained by a state-of-the-art leaderboard designed by the Streak Coding team
The correct answer would fetch you the link to the second round

Round 2: The Streak

The second round will consist of 11 coding questions of easy - medium difficulty hosted in the HackerRank platform
We would test here not how much difficult problems you are able to solve. Instead, we would look at how fast you are able to solve simple problems
The score for this round is your HackerRank score + the points you may have gathered in the first round
The top 6 teams at the end of this round would be chosen for the next round held the next day

Round 3: The Final Streak

The finalists will be given 7 coding questions hosted in the HackerRank platform
In this round, we will not only look at the speed of your problem solving ability, we will also see whether you are able to solve complex problems with efficient solutions
The leaderboard for this round would consider 30% of the last round and 70% of this round
The top two scorers will be given the title of "The Streak Coder of Ubertech19" along with a cash award
